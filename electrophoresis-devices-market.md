<p><strong>The Electrophoresis Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Electrophoresis Systems</li><li>Capillary Electrophoresis Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrophoresis devices market primarily includes gel electrophoresis systems and capillary electrophoresis systems. Gel electrophoresis systems use a gelatinous medium to separate biomolecules based on size, making them ideal for applications like DNA and protein analysis. In contrast, capillary electrophoresis systems use thin capillaries to enhance resolution and speed, allowing for the separation of smaller molecules and complex mixtures. Both systems play crucial roles in research, clinical diagnostics, and biopharmaceutical manufacturing, driving advancements in molecular biology and analytical chemistry.</p></p>
