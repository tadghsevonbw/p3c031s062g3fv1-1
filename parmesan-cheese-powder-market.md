<p><p>The Parmesan cheese powder market is divided into two main types: natural cheese powder and processed cheese powder. Natural cheese powder is derived from real Parmesan cheese, offering authentic flavor and quality, making it popular among gourmet food manufacturers and chefs. Processed cheese powder, on the other hand, is made from a blend of cheese and other ingredients, resulting in a more uniform taste and longer shelf life. This type is often used in snacks and convenience foods for its cost-effectiveness and ease of use.</p></p>
