<p><p>Eddy current sensors are categorized primarily into two types: split type and integrated type. Split type sensors feature a separate sensor and controller, allowing for flexible installation and easier maintenance, making them ideal for complex setups. Integrated type sensors, on the other hand, combine both sensor and controller into a single unit, which simplifies installation and reduces space requirements. Both types serve various industrial applications, including position measurement, thickness gauging, and non-contact detection of metallic objects.</p></p>
